Discover the Hidden Gems of Honnavar

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Honnavar, a coastal paradise blending serene landscapes and thrilling adventures. From the tranquil Sharavathi Backwaters to the enchanting Mangrove Boardwalk and the mesmerizing Apsarkonda Falls, Honnavar is a haven for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Places to Visit in Honnavara

Sharavathi Backwaters

Unwind amidst the serene beauty of the Sharavathi Backwaters. Whether you choose to cruise, kayak, or simply soak in the tranquil surroundings, this pristine destination promises a rejuvenating escape.

Mangrove Board Walk

Step into a natural wonderland at Honnavar's Mangrove Boardwalk. Meander through lush mangrove forests, observe unique ecosystems, and experience the peaceful harmony of nature at its best.
